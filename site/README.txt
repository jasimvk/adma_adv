This is a simple static site for Adma Advertising.

Pages:
- index.html (home)
- products.html (gallery)
- services.html
- contact.html

Add your product images into /site/images/ named p1.jpg .. p6.jpg and replace whatsapp-qr.jpg with your QR image.

To preview locally on macOS with Python 3 run:

  cd /Users/admin/adma_adv/site
  python3 -m http.server 8000

Then open http://localhost:8000 in your browser.
